Library-information Centre

Welcome to TJIT Library! TJIT Library is housed in an exclusive attractive spacious building next to Principal office and to Auditorium and It has tons of resources of VTU prescribed text books, Reference Books, standards and Hand Books in all the subject areas that are taught in our campus and offers many different types of services for your learning, teaching and research needs. It has good collection of educative CD-ROMs, Technical Reports, e-question papers etc for additional reference work. Previous years Bound volumes of periodicals are also maintained for cross reference works. It is fully computerized library with DELPLUS software for search the resources available with us. All staff members are well qualified in Library & Information Science to assist and guide the readers in locating the information they are looking for in the library. Separate Digital Library Facility with 30 + computer systems to access online resources of VTU consortium. Resource sharing service with other libraries is provided through DELNET membership.

Our Vision

TJIT Library inspires intellectual curiosity, facilitates learning, and serves as a catalyst for the creation of new knowledge. TJIT Library will be the leading organization in building and maintaining the information and knowledge resources in the field of engineering technology.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive high quality information resources and services in support of the research, teaching, and learning needs of the TJIT community. To bring the right information to the right person at the right time in the right format. The library remains open on all working days from 08.30 A.M. to 5.00 P.M. Academics, students and Staff can make use of the library above mentioned time.
